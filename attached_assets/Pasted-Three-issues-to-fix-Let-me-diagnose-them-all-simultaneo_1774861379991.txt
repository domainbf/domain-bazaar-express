Action needed in Supabase to remove the supabase.co link from emails:

Go to Supabase Dashboard → Authentication → Email Templates → Reset Password, and change the button URL from the default to:

{{ .SiteURL }}/auth/callback?token_hash={{ .TokenHash }}&type=recovery
The app now has an /auth/callback page that receives this link, verifies the token on your side, and redirects to the reset password form — the link in the email will show nic.rw instead of supabase.co.

Do the same for Confirm signup (change type to signup) and Change email (type email_change) if you want those links to use your domain too.
